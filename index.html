Foundations

Foundations is a 4 week pre-work remote course to get students up to speed with JavaScript basics and ready before the first day of school.

Ice Cream Social

The ice cream social, a Fullstack Academy tradition, is the first time students come visit the campus

Junior Phase

In the 6 week long junior phase, students learn concepts and technologies with the best instructors in the industry

During workshops in the junior phase, students pair-program to put the concepts they learned into practice

Review Week

During review week, the students take assessments to test their knowledge

"The review week assessments are not so hard, I am super prepared for this!!!

Review week is also a time for the students to relax and recharge

Fullstack students also like to go to hackathons during review week. Students have won over 18 hackathons.

Senior Phase

In the senior phase, students will participate in several group and individual projects to solidify their learning and to showcase on their resumes.

Every morning, the students will have a chance to practice whiteboarding code challenges in preparation for technical interviews.

Hiring Day

On hiring day, students will have a chance to showcase their months of hard work and demo their capstone projects to potential employers.

After the demos, the students will have a chance to interview with the employers in a round-robin style.

Graduation

"I graduated!"

Fullstack Flight

Post graduation, Fullstack provides students with job search assistance and salary negotiation mentoring.
